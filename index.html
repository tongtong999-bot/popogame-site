About Veritas Tech

Founded in late 2024, Veritas Tech is an innovative American startup headquartered in Delaware with an operations center in Los Angeles. Our core team consists of industry veterans from leaders like DJI, Sony, and Xiaomi.

We are a one-stop service center for handheld imaging, spanning R&D, manufacturing, and global logistics. Our mission is to unlock boundless creativity for everyone, everywhere through premium quality and competitive cost.
